Instructions

5 MX Switches
1 Microcontroller (RP2040 Pro Micro, Arduino Pro Micro, RP2040 Zero)
4 Heat Inserts 4.5 mm or less
4 M2 screws x 7mm
  Cables for the wiring, ethernet cables work fine
2 Dupont female Cables

You need to print:
1 case 
5 keycaps
1 plate 
1 bar 
1 microcontroller holder
